Novels

Books might be a real effort to read, sometimes. Films can be the easy way out but these two books have left me wanting for more.

The Millennium Series, by Stieg Larsson is hands-down the best crime novel series that I have ever read. It follows Lisbeth Salander, an asocial computer hacker with a photographic memory, and Mikael Blomkvist, an investigative journalist and publisher of a magazine called Millennium. Although, 10 books were planned in the series, it is such a shame that the author died a sudden death in 2004

Gone Girl, by Gilian Flynn honestly, don't bother about the film. This book is so thrilling and breathtaking that you just cannot keep it down. The use of unreliable narration combined with its plot twists and raging suspense makes this a wonder-read.
