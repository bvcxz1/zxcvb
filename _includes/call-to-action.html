About Me

I'm an Undergraduate Student Researcher in the Department of Physics at IIT Bombay. I am currently investigating candidates for Dark Matter through Gravitational Lensing observations and building a Star Tracker for Celestial Navigation. Although, a major part of my interest has been focussed on the study of Space Science and Technology, there is a 99% chance that you will find me anywhere from a History class to a debate on Etymology. I'm also an avid triathlete and enjoy playing the piano!

I received the Institute's Undergraduate Research Award for conducting exemplary research in the academic year 2020-21. Apart from the ones above, my research interests also include Computational Physics, Algorithm Design, Computer Architecture and the Theory of Computation
