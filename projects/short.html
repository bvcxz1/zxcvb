Other Short Projects

Project information

  • Fast Adder-based Arithmetic-Logic Unit Implementation: Designed an Arithmetic-Logic Unit (ALU) to perform signed addition and subtraction using Structural VHDL. The adder is based on the Kogge-Stone Architecture
  • Analysis of Meteorological Shapefiles: Analysed shapefiles and generated a spatial database of meteorological data collected from IMD, demonstrating spatial indexing and executing other operations
  • Motion-based Handwriting Recognition System: Modelled a novel method of input to enable writing in air or any surface as part of a team of 4 members over the course of the summer of 2020
  • Hyperspectral Image Processing: Processed 191 bands of hyperspectral imagery by averaging over the channels and performing histogram binning. Proceeded to perform an histogram equalisation to obtain a well-contrasted image and employed an unsupervised learning method to detect and extract spatial features from the dataset
  • General Astronomy: A reading project taken under the guidance of a mentor wherein I made a report containing an overview of the Big Bang theory, its foundation and observational support. I also performed some scientific computing in Astronomy on datafiles of images taken in outer space using astropy
  • Microcontroller Interfacing: Implemented UART and SPI communication protocols to achieve data transmission and sensor interfacing between two ATxmega microcontrollers using Embedded C on Atmel Studio
  • Spatial Interpolation to Predict Rainfall Patterns: Predicted rainfall patterns using various models ranging from spherical to exponential on the statistical computing programming language R
  • XLR8: Assembled a remote controlled bluetooth car capable of dodging obstacles and navigating a challenging circuit. A differential driving mechanism was implemented alongside the electrical and mechanical aspects of the bot
